OMBOK VILLAMOR

"I am forever inspired by the sea." Ombok Villamor is a visual artist who works in paint and sculpture. His artworks, both figurative and non-representational, resemble stylized marine life creatures from which he predominantly draws inspiration. He likens coral reefs to a sanctuary for every living creature on earth, which influences his choice of palette that is distinctly symphonic, deep, and vibrant.
